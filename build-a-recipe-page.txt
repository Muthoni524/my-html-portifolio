    <h1>Vanilla Recipe</h1>
    <p>recipe for a moist vanilla cake</p>  
    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Butter</li>
      <li>Sugar</li>
      <li>Eggs $ vanilla beans</li>
      <li>Self-raising flour</li>
      </ul> 
<h2>Instructions</h2>
<ol>
  <li>cream butter and sugar</li>
  <li>add eggs & vanilla pods to above mixture and stir</li>
  <li>add in the flour,mix if mix is not flowy add some milt bit by bit</li>
  <li>line baking tin,pour in mixture and bake in the oven</li>
</ol>
